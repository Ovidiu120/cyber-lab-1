# Phishing Email Analysis Report

## Summary
The email is a phishing attempt designed to steal user credentials by impersonating a trusted service.

## Indicators of Phishing
- Fake domain (paypaI-support.com)
- Suspicious link (non-official domain)
- Urgency and threat language
- Generic greeting

## Risk
High risk of credential theft.

## Recommendations
- Never click suspicious links
- Verify sender domain
- Enable MFA
- Report phishing emails
